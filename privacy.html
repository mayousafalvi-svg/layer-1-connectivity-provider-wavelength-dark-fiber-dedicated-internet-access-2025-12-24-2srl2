Privacy & Data Protection

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Awantrix Connectivity ("Awantrix", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards information when you visit our website, use our services, or otherwise interact with us.

By accessing or using our website or services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please discontinue use of our website and services.

1. Introduction & Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to information we collect through our website, communication channels (such as email and phone), and in the course of providing connectivity procurement and related services, including Dedicated Internet Access (DIA), wavelength, dark fiber, and other layer 1 connectivity solutions.

It does not apply to information processed solely by our carriers, partners, or other third-party providers under their own privacy policies, although we seek to work with providers that maintain appropriate data protection practices.

We process personal information as a "controller" when we determine the purposes and means of processing, and as a "processor" when handling information on behalf of our customers, in each case in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

2. Information We Collect

We collect both personal and non-personal information in order to operate our business, provide our services, and improve your experience. The types of information we may collect include:

2.1 Information you provide to us

  • Contact details such as name, job title, company name, email address, phone number, and mailing address.
  • Account and communication information, including messages you send us via forms, email, phone, or other channels.
  • Project and service details, such as site locations, bandwidth requirements, technical preferences, and other information necessary to provide quotes and deliver services.
  • Billing and contract information, where applicable, including purchase orders, invoicing details, and relevant contractual documentation.

2.2 Information collected automatically

When you visit our website or interact with our online services, we may automatically collect certain information using cookies and similar technologies. This can include:

  • Device and browser information (e.g., IP address, browser type and version, operating system, device identifiers).
  • Usage data, such as pages visited, time spent on pages, links clicked, referring URLs, and other diagnostic data.
  • Approximate location information derived from your IP address, where permitted by applicable law.

2.3 Information from third parties

We may receive information about you from third parties, such as our carriers, partners, publicly available sources, professional networking platforms, or event organizers, to the extent permitted by law. We use this information to help us maintain accurate records, understand prospective customer needs, and perform or improve our services.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for business and operational purposes, in each case in accordance with applicable law and, where required, based on appropriate legal bases such as consent, contract performance, legitimate interests, or legal obligations. These purposes include:

  • Providing and managing services. To respond to inquiries, prepare and deliver quotes, design connectivity solutions, manage procurement, coordinate installations, and provide ongoing support.
  • Communication. To contact you regarding your requests, send service-related updates, respond to questions, and provide customer support.
  • Improving our website and services. To analyze how our website and services are used, improve functionality, develop new offerings, and enhance user experience, including by leveraging AI-assisted tooling.
  • Marketing and business development. Where permitted, to send you information about our services, events, or content that may be of interest to you. You may opt out of receiving marketing communications at any time.
  • Security and fraud prevention. To protect our website, systems, and users from fraudulent or malicious activity, and to monitor, detect, and respond to potential security incidents.
  • Legal and compliance. To comply with applicable laws and regulations, respond to lawful requests and legal processes, and enforce our agreements and policies.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with third parties under the following circumstances:

  • Service providers and partners. With carriers, network operators, and other partners as necessary to source quotes, deliver connectivity services, and provide support; and with vendors that perform services on our behalf, such as hosting, analytics, communications, or security.
  • Business transfers. In connection with a merger, acquisition, reorganization, sale of assets, or similar transaction, where information may be transferred as part of the business assets.
  • Legal requirements. When we believe disclosure is necessary or appropriate to comply with applicable law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request; to protect the rights, property, or safety of Awantrix, our customers, or others; or to enforce our agreements.
  • With your consent. We may share information with third parties when you authorize or direct us to do so.

5. Cookies & Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies (such as pixels and local storage) to help operate our website, understand usage patterns, and improve performance. Cookies are small data files stored on your device when you visit a website.

5.1 Types of cookies we may use

  • Essential cookies: Required for core site functionality, such as security and basic navigation.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our site so we can improve content and usability.
  • Functional cookies: Remember your preferences and settings to deliver a more personalized experience.

5.2 Managing cookies

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings and, where available, through dedicated cookie management tools. Please note that blocking certain types of cookies may impact the functionality or performance of our website.

6. Data Security, Retention & Your Rights

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures designed to protect personal information against accidental or unlawful destruction, loss, alteration, unauthorized disclosure, or access. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Retention periods may vary depending on the nature of the data and applicable legal or contractual requirements.

Depending on your jurisdiction and subject to applicable law, you may have certain rights with respect to your personal information, such as the right to access, correct, update, restrict, or delete your data, or to object to certain processing or request data portability.

To exercise your rights or request more information, please contact us using the details in the Contact section below. We may need to verify your identity before responding to your request, and certain limitations or exemptions may apply.

7. International Data Transfers

Due to the global nature of connectivity services, your information may be transferred to, stored in, or accessed from countries other than the one in which it was originally collected, including jurisdictions that may have different data protection laws. Where required, we implement appropriate safeguards to protect personal information in connection with such transfers, such as standard contractual clauses or equivalent mechanisms recognized by applicable law.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technologies, legal requirements, or other factors. When we make material changes, we will update the "last updated" date at the top of the policy and, where appropriate, provide additional notice. Your continued use of our website or services after the effective date of any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
