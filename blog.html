Strategy Guide

Getting Started with Our Platform

Modern enterprises can’t treat connectivity as a commodity. This guide explains how to choose the right mix of Dedicated Internet Access, wavelength services, and dark fiber by understanding their roles across cost, control, and risk. It shows where each shines—from cloud-first branch networks to data-intensive multi–data center environments and long-term campus builds. You’ll learn how to map connectivity to real application flows, design for resilience with genuine path diversity, and navigate complex, geography-bound carrier options using carrier-neutral, AI-assisted procurement. The result: a future-proof, layered connectivity fabric that scales from 10 Mbps to 400 Gbps and beyond.
